Graphical Filters Plugin

Filters plugin allows to adjust video brightness, saturation and contrast.

Initialize player and plugins


<script>
	var player = videojs('player_1', {license: "key"});
	player.nuevo();
	player.filters(); 
</script>
You can access graphical filters dialog from settings menu. Additional on touch devices sparate brightness slider is available on left side of the player, just like you can see on Netflix player.

If you prefer to hide separate brightness slider, disable touchBrightness plugin's option. 
player.filters( touchBrightness:false );
If you prefer to hide filters item in settings menu, but keep brightness slider on touch devices: 
player.filters({ filtersMenu:false });
